Inspiration

Virat Kohli's journey from a young boy with a passion for cricket to becoming one of the world's greatest batsmen is truly inspiring. His dedication, hard work, and never-give-up attitude have made him a role model for aspiring cricketers worldwide.

Despite facing numerous challenges and setbacks, Kohli's resilience and determination have seen him rise to the pinnacle of international cricket. His leadership qualities and ability to perform under pressure have earned him accolades and respect from peers and fans alike.




"Self-belief and hard work will always earn you success."

Biography

Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer and the former captain of the India national team. He is widely regarded as one of the best batsmen in the world. Born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, Kohli started his career in cricket at a young age and has since achieved numerous records and accolades.

Kohli made his ODI debut in August 2008 and quickly became known for his aggressive batting style and consistency. He has been honored with several prestigious awards, including the ICC Cricketer of the Year, and has led India to numerous victories across all formats of the game.
